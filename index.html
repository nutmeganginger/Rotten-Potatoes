Composting.

It's easier than you think.

Food takes up more space in U.S. landfills than anything else. we can change that!

Composting is a form of magic. It has the power to transform vegetable peelings into nutrient-rich soil and help plants grow. It’s also relatively simple to do.

Reducing food waste is the #1 solution to the climate crisis, according to Project DrawDown – coming above electric cars, solar power and plant-based diets.
